Pioneering Global Food Security

Caltation is a technology-focused nonprofit working to build sustainable food systems globally by empowering communities with tools and knowledge for lasting food security.
Through innovation and collaboration, Caltation aims to combat hunger by making nutritious food accessible to vulnerable populations around the world.
  • Increased crop yields through electroculture
  • Community access to fresh, nutritious food
  • Education on sustainable urban agriculture
