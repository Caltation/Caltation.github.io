Frequently asked questions

Can’t find the answer you’re looking for? Reach out to our customer support team.

How does Caltation combat global hunger?
Caltation combats global hunger through sustainable, technology-driven solutions that empower individuals and communities worldwide.
How does Caltation leverage technology to address hunger?
Caltation leverages cutting-edge technology to create a comprehensive, scalable ecosystem that addresses the root causes of hunger.
How can I get involved with Caltation's mission?
You can get involved by donating directly through our cloud-based platform or by volunteering with local food vendors and community organizations.
What is Caltation's approach to sustainable agriculture?
Caltation's approach includes developing advanced urban agriculture systems that incorporate novel electroculture techniques to boost crop yields and support community gardens.
How does Caltation promote education and knowledge-sharing?
Caltation serves as a hub for educational resources, best practices, and success stories to inspire and equip communities to adopt sustainable agricultural practices.
